What information we collect and how we use it

Web Forms

The main method for us collecting data is through our contact forms. Here you will supply your name and email address so that we can respond to your messages. Our contact forms use a third party service called Formspree, subject to its Privacy policy.

Note that Formspree.io only saves the most recent 100 messages. After that, messages are deleted. Formspree.io sends emails to us through Sendgrid.com in accordance with the Sendgrid.com Privacy Policy.

We use Mailchimp as our newsletter service. In order to sign up for the newsletter you are required to give them your email address. All emails contain an unsusbcribe link which you can use at any time in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Who might we share your data with?

  • Google Analytics. We share your IP address, and browsing behaviour, with analytics and search engine providers that assist us in the improvement and optimisation of our site.
  • Yandex.Metrica. It provides us with anonymized information about our users (such as IP address, browser version, and so on) and how they use the Site.
  • Google Fonts. It is a font hosting service that allows us to easily serve unique fonts to our visitors.
  • Social networks and other websites. Our website includes links to other websites, whose privacy practices may be different from ours. If you submit Personal Information to any of those sites, your information is governed by their privacy policies. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policy of any website you visit.

We will not share or sell the identifiable personal information we collect or generate except as defined in this section.

Cookies

On the Site, only Google Analytics and Yandex.Metrica set cookies. Again, if you have Do Not Track activated in your browser, we don’t collect any information and won’t let Google and Yandex set any cookies in your browser.

In general, you can block cookies by using your browser’s setting, or by installing a browser add-on such as Privacy Badger or uBlock Origin.

Updating your personal information or revoking consent

The GDPR gives European visitors certain rights over their data. We are happy to extend these same rights to everyone, worldwide.

These rights give you the ability to verify and seek rectification of your data, delete data, export data and more. If you would like to do this, please contact us at vladimir.nikishin[at]gmail.com.
